Are we going to have access to the Letter To Parent (LTP) in Spanish

Yes. Once you have filled out all the required fields for the LTP, and it is approved by the Bureau, you will be able to go into Reports, select Letter to Parent and you will be able to see two icons you could click on: Spanish or English.

We don’t know the reimbursement rates yet, how are we going to determine the prices?

Reimbursement rates will be posted in the Resources section in the upper bar of the screen as soon as they are available from USDA.

How are we going to do the Letter to Parent if the Board of Education hasn’t determined the lunch prices by June 1st?

You can complete the LTP anytime after June 1, as soon as prices are determined. The Letter to Parent must be approved before you can complete the other application documents.

When do I submit the board approved minutes for the prices that will be on the Letter to Parent?

We do not require copies of minutes for the Letter to Parent prices.

What is the timeline for the Letter to Parent approval?

You should receive a response from the Bureau in approximately one week

Who gets notified once the Letter to Parent is approved and how?

Certifier, Alt, Certifier, Submitter and Alt. Submitter will get an email notifying them that their packet has been approved.
